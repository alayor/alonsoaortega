Minisuper
Minisuper is a disruptive technology solution focused on making efficient purchases of basic foods like tortillas, fruits, canned goods, soft drinks, etc.
With our mobile app, consumers order a product whose order is assigned to the best ranking deliverer based on their proximity, reputation, and offered price. Minisuper charges an access fee to deliverers, which is calculated based on their weekly sales.
Technologies used: ES6, React, React Native, Koa, Knex, Docker, Socket.IO, Cucumber BDD.
